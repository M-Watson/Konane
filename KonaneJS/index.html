How to Play

  • Setup: Alternating black and white pieces fill the board.
  • Phase 1 (Black): Remove one piece from the center or corners.
  • Phase 2 (White): Remove one piece adjacent to the empty spot.
  • Phase 3 (Jumping): Take turns jumping over an opponent's piece into an empty space.
  • Jumps are horizontal or vertical. Only single jumps per turn in this version.
  • Winning: The last player able to make a move wins.
